About Us

Small team. Enterprise-grade output.

Anchovy Labs is an AI-native product studio founded to prove that small teams, augmented by AI, can ship enterprise-grade software at startup speed. We build practical iOS products with strong privacy defaults and production-grade engineering discipline.

Our Approach

We use multi-agent orchestration to accelerate implementation, automated testing to maintain quality, and rapid iteration cycles grounded in measurable user behavior. The result is fast delivery without sacrificing technical rigor.
