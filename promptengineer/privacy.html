Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

PromptEngineer ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

On-Device Processing Only

All speech recognition and prompt generation happens entirely on your device. Your voice and your ideas never leave your iPhone.

Information We Collect

  • Voice input: Processed locally via Apple's Speech framework. Never transmitted.
  • Prompt history: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Analytics: None. No tracking, no telemetry.

Third-Party Services

PromptEngineer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cloud services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

PromptEngineer does not knowingly collect information from anyone. No account is required.

Contact

Changes
