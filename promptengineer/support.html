Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a prompt? Tap the microphone and speak naturally. PromptEngineer structures your words into a clean prompt automatically.
  • Does it require an internet connection? No. All speech recognition and processing happens entirely on your device — no internet required, ever.
  • How do I use templates? Tap the Templates tab, choose a template category, then start recording. The template shapes the output automatically.
  • Where is my prompt history? Tap the History tab to view all your saved prompts. You can copy or share any prompt from there.
  • What AI models does it work with? Any — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any LLM. PromptEngineer produces clean text prompts you paste wherever you like.
  • What data does the app collect? Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no cloud. Your prompts stay on your device.
