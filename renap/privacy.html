Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Renap ("the App") is committed to your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

What We Collect

Renap collects nothing. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no crash reporters that send data off-device, and no advertising networks.

  • Conversion history: Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted.
  • Favorites: Stored locally. Never transmitted.
  • Currency rates: Fetched from a public exchange-rate API (numbers only — no personal data is sent).

Data Storage

All data is stored on-device and protected by iOS data protection (NSFileProtectionComplete).

Third-Party Services

Renap does not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Renap does not knowingly collect information from anyone. No account is required.

Contact

Questions? support@anchovylabs.ai

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
