Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I convert units? Tap any category from the home grid, select your source and target units, then type a value. Results update instantly as you type.
  • How do I save a favorite conversion? Long-press any conversion pair in the converter to pin it. It will appear in your Favorites tab for one-tap access.
  • Does Renap work offline? Yes — all unit conversions work offline. Currency rates require an occasional internet connection to stay current.
  • Where is my conversion history? Tap the History tab to see all past conversions. You can tap any entry to re-run it.
  • What categories are supported? Length, mass, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, energy, pressure, currency, and data storage.
  • Is my data private? Yes. Everything stays on your device. Renap has no accounts, no analytics, and no data collection.
