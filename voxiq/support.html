FAQ

  • How does VoxIQ work? Record your idea, review/edit transcript text, then generate a structured prompt.
  • Does VoxIQ work offline? Core voice transcription and prompt shaping run on-device.
  • What does Pro unlock? Unlimited daily prompt generations and custom template creation.
  • Do free users get templates? Yes. Built-in templates are available on the free tier.

Troubleshooting

  • Microphone not working? iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Microphone -> enable VoxIQ.
  • Speech recognition not working? iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Speech Recognition -> enable VoxIQ.
  • Purchase not restored? Open VoxIQ paywall and tap Restore Purchase.
