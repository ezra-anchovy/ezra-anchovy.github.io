VoxIQ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

VoxIQ is designed to keep your data on your device.

1. On-Device Processing

VoxIQ processes speech and prompt generation on-device using Apple frameworks.

2. Microphone and Speech Recognition

  • Microphone: Used only while recording voice input.
  • Speech Recognition: Converts speech to text for prompt generation.
  • No audio uploads: VoxIQ does not send recorded audio to third-party servers.

3. Local Storage

Generated prompts, templates, and history are stored locally on your device.

4. Data Collection and Tracking

VoxIQ does not use analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking frameworks.
