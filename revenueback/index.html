Frequently Asked Questions

How do you compare to Churnkey, Baremetrics, or Gravy?

We're 5-20x more affordable while delivering comparable or better recovery rates (68% average). Churnkey starts at $250/month, Baremetrics requires custom pricing, Gravy needs an audit call—and we're $29-$99/month with a 7-day free trial.

How is this different from Stripe's recovery?

Stripe is a payment processor, not a recovery specialist. They send generic emails that often go to spam. We use high-deliverability plain-text sequences and advanced timing based on our study of 340+ merchants—resulting in nearly double the recovery rate.

Do I need a developer to set this up?

Zero engineering required. Click "Connect Stripe," authorize via OAuth, and you're live. Most users complete setup in under 3 minutes. No API keys, no webhooks, no code changes.

Is it safe? What about data security?

Yes. We use Stripe Connect (OAuth)—the same authentication used by Stripe Partners. We never see your customers' full card details, have read-only access to your records, and are SOC-2 and GDPR compliant.

Do you take a percentage of recovered revenue?

Never. We charge a flat monthly fee—$29, $49, or $99—regardless of how much revenue we recover. You keep 100% of recovered payments. That's how confident we are in our results.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel anytime with one click. No contracts, no minimum terms, no hidden fees. If you're not happy, we don't want your money—it's that simple.

Will this annoy my customers?

No. Because we send plain-text, helpful messages from your domain, they feel like a supportive account manager checking in—not a collections agency. Our unsubscribe rate is under 2%.
