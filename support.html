Support

FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app settings and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the original Apple ID.

Where can I report a bug?

Email support@anchovylabs.ai with steps to reproduce and your iOS version.

Do your apps work offline?

Core app features are available offline; cloud sync features require internet access.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Send feature requests by email and include your workflow so we can evaluate fit.

How is my data protected?

We minimize data collection, use secure transport, and process privacy requests promptly.

How fast is support response?

We typically reply within 1-2 business days.
