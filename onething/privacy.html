Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

One Thing ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

One Thing is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Your Habits: Stored locally on your device only
  • Usage Data: None (no analytics, no tracking)
  • Personal Data: None required

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your habit information never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

One Thing does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party SDKs

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

One Thing is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally:

  • Delete the app to delete all data
  • No account needed = no data to export

Contact

For privacy questions: support@onething.app

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
