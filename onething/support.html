Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a new habit? Tap the + button on the main screen. Enter your habit and save.
  • How do I mark a habit complete? Tap the circle next to your habit. It will fill in to show completion.
  • What happens to my data? All your data stays on your device. We never see it.
  • Can I sync between devices? Not yet — this is planned for a future update.
  • Is there a watch app? Yes! One Thing includes a watchOS companion app.
  • What if I need to skip a day? Long-press on your habit to intentionally skip. Rest is valid!
