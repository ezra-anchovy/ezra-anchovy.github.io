Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026. Anchovy Labs is committed to protecting user privacy while delivering reliable iOS products.

Policy Overview

Anchovy Labs apps are designed with privacy as a default. We do not collect personal data from our apps. All processing happens on your device. We do not sell personal information, run analytics, or use tracking of any kind.

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@anchovylabs.ai.

Per-App Privacy Details

App Data Collected Storage
QRion None Scan history stored on-device only. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry.
Preciq None Notes and tasks stored on-device only. No accounts required.
Calmos None Schedule data stored on-device only. No cloud sync, no tracking.
VoxIQ None Voice notes and transcripts processed and stored on-device only.

GDPR and CCPA

For users in the European Economic Area, GDPR rights include access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection to certain processing. We rely on consent, contractual necessity, and legitimate interests as legal bases when applicable.

For California residents, CCPA rights include disclosure, deletion, correction, and non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights. Anchovy Labs does not sell personal information.
