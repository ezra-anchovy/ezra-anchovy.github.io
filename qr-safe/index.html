QRSafe

Scan, generate, and share QR codes — privately and securely.

Features

  • Instant Scanning — Point your camera at any QR code for immediate results.
  • Generate QR Codes — Create codes for URLs, text, WiFi, contacts, and more.
  • Scan History — Keep a searchable log of everything you've scanned.
  • Share & Export — Share generated codes as images or export to PDF.
  • Privacy First — All data stays on your device. No analytics, no tracking.
