Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I scan a QR code? Open QRSafe and point your camera at any QR code. Results appear instantly.
  • How do I generate a QR code? Tap the Generate tab, choose a type (URL, text, WiFi, etc.), enter your content, and tap Create.
  • Where is my scan history stored? All scan history is stored locally on your device. We never see or upload it.
  • Can I export QR codes? Yes — tap Share on any generated code to save as an image or export to PDF (Pro feature).
  • What does QRSafe Pro include? Pro unlocks PDF export, Pro Vault storage, and unlimited history. Upgrade from Settings.
  • Is my data private? Yes. QRSafe stores everything on-device. No analytics, no tracking, no cloud sync.
