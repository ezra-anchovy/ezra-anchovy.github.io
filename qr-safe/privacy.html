Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

QRSafe ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

QRSafe is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data:

  • Scanned QR Codes: Stored locally on your device only
  • Generated QR Codes: Stored locally on your device only
  • Camera Access: Used only for scanning — never recorded or transmitted
  • Usage Data: None (no analytics, no tracking)
  • Personal Data: None required

Data Storage

All data — scan history, generated codes, favorites — is stored locally on your device. Your information never leaves your device.

Camera Usage

QRSafe accesses your camera only to scan QR codes. Camera input is processed in real-time on-device and is never recorded, stored as video, or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

QRSafe does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

QRSafe is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally:

  • Delete the app to delete all data
  • No account needed = no data to export

Contact

For privacy questions: support@anchovylabs.ai

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
